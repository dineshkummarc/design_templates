ABOUT ME

Let me introduce myself better!

From a Farmer Son to a Software Engineer, Designer...

I started my business career from an industry that despite the small connection to graphic design, it is directly linked to …creativity! I graduated from a “Goldsmith’s School” and while designing and creating jewels for the family business for a few years, I realized that drawing & designing was my favorite part. The decision to quit this job and start over as a graphic designer was only a matter of time. I took some seminar courses and in no time I was employed in various advertising agencies as a graphic and web designer for the next 7 years gaining lots of experience!

A small Secret...

Back in 2008, it was time for something even more challenging... I decided to start my own business with my partner in life and work and after some "brainstorming" we took the decision to name our business "E T" which was indeed our very own "secret" for quite some time. E T is a creative design studio based in California. Currently the business has grown to a Corporation and is for USA only (I intend to make an international version as well) but in the meantime feel free to visit my business website here.
